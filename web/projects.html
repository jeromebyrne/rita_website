RagCat

This was a final year college project developed with 4 other programmers (Gavin Campbell, Liam Rudel, Simon Coulter and Conor Masterson. Art by Paul Lohman. Music by Darragh Coy).

It was developed in c++ and used the havok physics middleware along with Ogre 3D for rendering.

The aim of the game is to use the telekinetic powers of a paralysed cat to reach the goal area. The project went on to win $10,000 in the Intel/Havok Physics World Cup.

Trampenstein!

A Physics demo created in OpenGL and C++ for college.

Realtime spring simulation with basic sphere collisions. The trampoline can be controlled by the user, applying directional forces to the centre spring.
